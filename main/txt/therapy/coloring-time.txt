Children spent hours with coloring books. But coloring books aren't just for kids any more. Adults love them, too.

It's easy to have a coloring hobby. Many adult coloring books are available today. There are dozens of themes to choose from. They are sold at many different stores. They are also sold on the Internet.

Children may color as part of playtime. Adults may color as a hobby. Some adults say that coloring makes them calm. It can be a way to reduce stress.

Crayons are not the only way to go. Colored pencils are popular with adults. Felt tip markers are another medium to use. Ink pens add a nice touch.

Adult coloring books are a great gift. They just may bring out the kid in all of us.
