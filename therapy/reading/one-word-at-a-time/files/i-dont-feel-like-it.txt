After a stroke, getting better is a long journey. It's hard and slow. Sometimes, it makes us feel angry. Here are three ways to help you stay motivated.

Try to be positive. This can be challenging. Every day think of 3 things that make you happy or grateful. Say them aloud or write them down.

Set small goals. Sure, you want to be back to "normal." But what does that mean? What's the first step? Is is dressing yourself? Ordering your own meal? Walking? Take that goal and break it down further.

Help someone out. When we can't do our usual work, we can feel useless. Helping others makes us feel useful. It gives us a purpose. Find simple ways to help others.
